# Thanks

pota is a research and personal project built on top of other people's
hard work.

## Solid

The API, base components and compiler are heavily inspired by
[Solid](https://www.solidjs.com/) and
[dom-expressions](https://github.com/ryansolid/dom-expressions). pota
differs to an extent based on personal preference, but the lineage is
direct.

## voby, oby & flimsy

The reactive core used to be pluggable, and these libraries — among
other attempts — were explored as backends. The pluggable idea was
eventually dropped, but the experiments shaped what pota ships today.
[voby](https://github.com/vobyjs/voby) ·
[oby](https://github.com/vobyjs/oby) ·
[flimsy](https://github.com/fabiospampinato/flimsy).
